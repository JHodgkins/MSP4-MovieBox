Add a movie product guidelines

To add a movie title to MovieBox, please follow these steps.

  • Supply a valid full movie title, for example:
    Star Ware - The Phanthom Menace
  • Description:
    Use imdb to look up the title and use the Plot as the description.
  • Actors, directedby, running time and imdb rating
    This informaation can be gained through imdb when looking up the title.
  • Certificate
    The certificate can change across countries, check the media its self to establish if it is a PAL or NTSC version (UK/US) and apply the correct certification in the field.

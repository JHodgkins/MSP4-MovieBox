Need a way to archive your Movie collection

Why not try Movie2Archive, a simple to use media archive platform, perfect for the budding movie collector or use it for organising your existing collection of movie titles.

here how simple it is to get started!

Register and create your movie2archive account, or login if you already have an account

Visit the 'My collection' page and add a movie, you only need the movie title, media type and edition and then the location of your movie.

View a simple list of your movie collection or view each title to discover more information about your movie.
